The Vision Behind the Lens

The world doesn't need to be posed to look beautiful. I believe the entire landscape is my studio, and the most compelling images come from simply paying attention to daily life.

Operating purely from Chennai, I focus on capturing unposed, raw moments. My photography is defined by dark, dramatic lighting and rich cinematic colors that turn sightseeing and urban sights into timeless visual narratives.
